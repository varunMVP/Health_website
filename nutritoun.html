Green Smoothie Bowl

Start your day with this nutrient-packed smoothie bowl!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds

Instructions:

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Pour into a bowl and top with your favorite fruits and nuts.
