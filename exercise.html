Exercise Tips

Start with a Warm-up

Always begin your workout with a 5-10 minute warm-up to prepare your body for exercise and reduce the risk of injury.

  • Light jogging or marching in place
  • Arm circles
  • Leg swings
  • Gentle stretches

Mix Cardio and Strength Training

For a well-rounded fitness routine, include both cardiovascular exercise and strength training in your workouts.

  • Aim for 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio per week
  • Include strength training exercises 2-3 times per week
  • Allow rest days between strength training sessions for muscle recovery
