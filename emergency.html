Emergency Services

In Case of Emergency

If you are experiencing a life-threatening emergency, please call your local emergency number immediately.

When to Call Emergency Services:

  • Chest pain or difficulty breathing
  • Uncontrolled bleeding
  • Sudden or severe pain
  • Coughing up or vomiting blood
  • Severe burns
  • Suspected stroke or heart attack
  • Serious accidents or injuries

Remember, if you're unsure whether the situation is a true emergency, it's better to err on the side of caution and call for help.
